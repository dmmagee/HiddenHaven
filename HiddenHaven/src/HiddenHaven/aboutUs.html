Our Goal At Hidden Haven

We got our start out on this farm back in 2005 just with a single horse belonging to our daughter Breanna. At the beginning, Bre and her horse Mirra had the whole farm to themselves. We soon realized the joy we had raising her horse on this farm, and in 2006 we start this boarding company. Which in turn became the company we are today!

Our goal here at Hidden Haven is provide the best boarding facility for your horse in all of Central Oregon. We strive to maintain a fun and clean boarding environment that both you are your horse will enjoy! We offer competitive boarding prices and we challenge any other facilities to match our level of in depth friendship offered by boarding with us!

If you're considering boarding your horse in Central Oregon, come drop us a visit! Located only a stones throw away from the Bend Airport.
