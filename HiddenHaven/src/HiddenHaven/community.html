4H Fundraising Cocktail Party!

by Hidden Haven Horse Care & Boarding

Come meet us at the ranch for our summer cocktail party! Who doesn't like pouring booze down their throats with horses and close friends! All are boarders and families are invited. We will be providing free food, and 100% of all drink purchases will go directly to helping support 4H for kids!
