How many acres of land do you have available for boarding?

"Our home and farm is nestled on the outskirts of Bend on a 4.89 acre property. More than 4 acres of our land is completely dedicated to caring and housing our boarders."

What are your pricing packages?

"Our pricing packages break down simply into two catagories:

Package #1 - $350 to board your horse here, and we take complete care of your horse. To include, but not limited to: Feeding, Turning out to Pasture, Cleaning their corral, & tending to 24 hour emergency aid.
Package #2 - $250 to board your horse here, but the difference from Package #1 being you will be entirely responsible for keeping your horses corral tidy."

Can we ride horses naked at this facility?

"Nope."

Can we launch fireworks at this facility?

"Nope."

How many loafing sheds does Hidden Haven provide?

"Currently Hidden Haven has seven loafing sheds."

How are the horses fed?

"Here at Hidden Haven, our staff feeds all the horses twice a day. Generally around 8:00am and again at 6:00pm. We feed each horse roughly 10 pounds of hay each meal, unless you negotiate for more hay. All of our water troughs are filled every other day, and all of our water troughs have inlaid heaters to prevent freezing in cold temperatures."

Can I call other horses names if they neigh at me meanly?

"Nope. We encourage a loving environment here at Hidden Haven. If you find yourself tempted to call a horse names, turn the other cheek and pet the horse nicely on the mane. They love that."
