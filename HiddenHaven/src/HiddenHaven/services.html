What we offer here at Hidden Haven

List of services we provide:

Feeding Your Horses

We feed our horses twice a day, typically at 8:00am and at 5:30pm. We only feed the horses with top notch, weed free quality hay, from a trusted hay provider here in Central Oregon. We maintain full water troughs by filling them every other day, and cleaning them out thorughly every other week. We feed all the horses here out of large tractor tires, to ensure separate eating conditions and to prevent the wind from blowing their hay away.

For The Horses

Currently we have 8 lots and 2 pastures to secure our horses in. Of the 8 lots, we have 7 loafing sheds to shelter your horse from the elements, and ensure your horses food remains ready and dry. We have two Tac & Gear sheds to stow all of your gear you could need to saddle up or care for your horse. We also feature a large training arena to trot, gallop, and jump. Train with your horse with our provided barrels and jumping beams. Also to include a fully stocked horse washing station. Lastly, we have a round pin to prep your horse for training and submission.

24 Hour Dedicated Aid

Although we are NOT a licensed doctor service, if you allow us, we have years of experience dealing with many types of issues regarding horse wellness. We have many common medicines on hand to be able to quickly respond to nearly any situation that could arise. There has been nearly countless nights we have stayed up during the night to moniter horses that were colicing, and occasionaly offered completely free medication with the boarders permission to administer it on the spot. Our promise is to care for your horse as if they were our own children, and to help unconditionally.
